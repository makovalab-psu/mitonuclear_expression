Aim: Calculate mitonuclear DNA discordance (MND).

Step 1: Obtain global (nuclear DNA) ancestry estimates for the GTEx samples.

Step 2: Obtain mtDNA haplogroup for GTEx samples.

Step 3: Determine the fraction of the (nuclear) global ancestry that does not match the ancestry of mtDNA.

		The python script "calc_MND_K2.py" takes the global ancestry and mtDNA haplogroup files and returns this calculation of mitonuclear DNA discordance for K=2.
